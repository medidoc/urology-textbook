Links in Urology and Medicine

Urological Journals

BJU International: journal of the British Association of Urological Surgeons. Free full text 1 year after publication.

European Urology: official journal of the EAU, full-text only with subscription.

Journal of Urology: official journal of the AUA, full-text only with subscription.

Der Urologe: official publication of the DGU and BDU (German), full-text only with subscription.

Urology: official journal of the Societe Internationale d'Urologie, full-text only with subscription.

Interdisciplinary Journals

Ärzteblatt: official journal of the Bundesärztekammer and Kassenärztliche Bundervereinigung (German), free full text.

The British Journal of Radiology: Free full text 2 years after publication.

Cell: molecular biology, free full text 1 year after publication.

The Cochrane Collaboration: full-text only with subscription..

JAMA: Journal of the American Medical Association. Free full text 6 months after publication and registration.

Nature: is a British multidisciplinary scientific journal and one of the world's top academic journals. It covers a wide range of scientific fields.

New England Journal of Medicine: Journal of the Massachusetts Medical Society. Free full text 6 months after publication and registration.

The Lancet: partial free full text after registration.

Radiology: journal of the Radiological Society of North America.

Science: is the academic journal of the American Association for the Advancement of Science (AAAS) and one of the world's top academic journals.

Online portal for Springer journals: i.g. Der Internist, Anaesthesist, Urologe, Chirurg ... Full text requires subscription.

Urology Resources on the Internet

American Urology Association: clinical guidelines.

European Association of Urology: clinical guidelines.

European Guidelines of sexually transmitted infections, provided by the International Union against Sexually Transmitted Infections (IUSTI)

Nomograms of the Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center: calculates the prognosis for prostate cancer, bladder carcinoma and renal cell carcinoma.

Prostate Cancer Risk Calculator: calculates the prostate cancer risk based on the ERSPC study data and uses Age, family history, LUTS and PSA.

German Urology guidelines: the urological guidelines are available via the site navigation. Provided by the Arbeitsgemeinschaft der Wissenschaftlichen Medizinischen Fachgesellschaften (AWMF).

Interdisciplinary Medical Information on the Internet

CT is us: very useful website covering CT, application protocols and case studies of spiral CT, CT-Angiography and PET.

PubMed : is a service of the National Library of Medicine that includes over 16 million citations from MEDLINE and other life science journals for biomedical articles back to the 1950s.

Virtual Slidebox: great site for virtual microscopy from the University of Iowa. After selecting a virtual microscope slide, every area in maximum magnification be viewed on the slide. There is a wide range of normal histology slides and histopathology slides.

webpath pathology: very good and extensive website about gross pathology and histology, from the Florida State University College of Medicine.

Associations, Societies and Foundations

AUA: American Urological Association.

DGU and BDU: shared website of the Deutsche Gesellschaft für Urologie and Berufsverband Deutscher Urologen.

EAU: European Association of Urology.

ICS: International Continence Society

National Kidney Foundation (NKF): The National Kidney Foundation, a major nonprofit voluntary health organization, is dedicated to kidney and urinary preventing tract diseases, improving the health and well-being of individuals and families affected by kidney disease and increasing the availability of all organs for transplantation.
