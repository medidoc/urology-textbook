Signs and Symptoms of Urological Diseases

From Signs and Symptoms to Diagnosis

The knowledge of the various causes for signs and symptoms is the basis to target further studies and to quickly find the right diagnosis. The diagnosis stays, due to an error probability, a differential diagnosis. It is however the basis for the initiation of the treatment. As long as the healing process continues, the diagnosis must be questioned and other differential diagnosis must be kept in mind.

  • Acute abdominal pain or flank pain
  • Adrenal tumor
  • Dermatosis with genital or inguinal manifestation
  • Fever
  • Genital ulcer
  • Gynecomastia
  • Hematospermia
  • Hematuria
  • Hydronephrosis
  • Inguinal lymphadenopathy (groin lumps)
  • LUTS
  • Nocturia
  • Penile tumor
  • Pneumaturia
  • Proteinuria
  • Renal tumor
  • Scrotal swelling
  • Urethritis
  • Urinary frequency
  • Urinary incontinence
  • Urinary retention
  • Urinoma
